About CSC

CSC was founded in 2025 with one goal: to be a place worth returning to. We source our beans with the same care a good engineer brings to dependencies — deliberate, traceable, and always worth the weight.

Whether you're here to think, to work, or to simply sit with a good cup, we built this space for you.

Coffee & Code Trivia

Which programming language was named after coffee?

Java. Released in 1995 by Sun Microsystems, the language was named after Java coffee from Indonesia. The cup on its logo was no accident. (InfoWorld)
